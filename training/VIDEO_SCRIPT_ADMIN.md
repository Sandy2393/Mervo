# Video Script: Admin Walkthrough (5–6 min)

**Audience:** Customer admins

1) Intro (0:00–0:20)
- Who it's for, what they'll learn.

2) Login & Security (0:20–0:50)
- Show login, profile, enable 2FA.

3) Company & Team Setup (0:50–1:40)
- Create company profile, invite admins and contractors.
- Permissions overview (roles, least privilege reminder).

4) Jobs Lifecycle (1:40–3:20)
- Create job with rate and due date.
- Assign contractor, status transitions (open → in_progress → completed).
- Attachments and notes.

5) Timesheets & Ratings (3:20–4:20)
- Review timesheets, export CSV.
- Submit contractor rating.

6) Notifications & Reports (4:20–5:00)
- Email/in-app notifications.
- Quick reports and dashboards.

7) Wrap (5:00–5:30)
- Where to get help (support@mervo.app), docs, community.
- CTA: try creating first job.

**B-roll / overlays:** Highlight buttons, toasts, and success states.
**Callouts:** Security, data privacy, and rollback plan link.
