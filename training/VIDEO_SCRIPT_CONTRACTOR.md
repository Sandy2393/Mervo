# Video Script: Contractor Workflow (3–4 min)

**Audience:** Field contractors

1) Intro (0:00–0:15)
- What they'll do: accept jobs, clock time, submit proof.

2) Login & Profile (0:15–0:45)
- Login, update profile, add specialties.

3) Accepting Jobs (0:45–1:30)
- View available jobs, accept/decline, see rate and due date.

4) On the Job (1:30–2:30)
- Clock-in/out, add notes, upload photos.
- Offline mode reminder if app supports it.

5) Submit & Complete (2:30–3:00)
- Submit completion, see status updates.

6) Earnings & Support (3:00–3:30)
- View earnings, where to get help, who to contact.

**Callouts:** Keep location services on if required; ensure photos upload before closing app.
