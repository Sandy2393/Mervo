# Admin Workshop Plan (90 minutes)

## Goals
- Admins can configure companies, jobs, contractors
- Admins can review audits, timesheets, and exports

## Agenda
- 0–10m: Overview & roles
- 10–25m: Settings tour (companies, teams, permissions)
- 25–45m: Jobs lifecycle (create → assign → complete)
- 45–60m: Timesheets, ratings, and exports
- 60–75m: Notifications, email templates, access control
- 75–90m: Q&A and common troubleshooting

## Hands-On Exercises
- Create a company and invite an admin
- Create a job with due date and rate
- Assign contractor and track status
- Export timesheets CSV

- Slides: `handover/ADMIN_TRAINING_SLIDES.md`
- Demo data: `demo/seed_data.sql`, `demo/generate_demo_data.ts`
