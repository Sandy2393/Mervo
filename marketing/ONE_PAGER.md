# Mervo One-Pager

## What is Mervo?
Field operations platform for companies managing contractors and jobs. Mobile-first, offline-friendly, and fast to onboard.

## Who it serves
- Operations managers with distributed contractors
- Admins who need auditability, payroll exports, and quick reporting
- Contractors needing simple job intake, clock-in/out, and proof uploads

## Key Benefits
- Faster job dispatch and completion (offline-safe)
- Reduced disputes with photo/timeproof trails
- Payroll-ready exports and integrations
- Secure by design (RLS, audit logs, SSO-ready)

## Features
- Job creation, assignment, and status tracking
- Clock-in/out with notes and photo uploads
- Timesheets and ratings
- Admin dashboards and reports
- API + Postman collection

## Pricing Snapshot
- Basic: $0–$199/mo, email support
- Standard: $499–$1,499/mo, Slack support
- Premium: $2,999–$7,999/mo, 24/7 + CSM
