# DPA Template (APP_ID)

- Parties: Controller (Customer) and Processor (APP_ID).
- Purpose: provide APP_ID services (APP_TAG).
- Data: contractor info, photos, reports, job metadata; no payment card data stored by default.
- Security: encryption in transit/at rest, access controls, RLS, logging.
- Subprocessors: list maintained; notify before changes.
- Data subject rights: assist with access/rectification/deletion; respond within SLA.
- Transfers: use SCCs or equivalent where required.
- Retention/deletion: delete or return data upon termination; backups pruned per schedule.
- Breach notice: notify without undue delay; include scope, impact, remediation.
