# Customer Feedback Capture (APP_ID)

- Channels: in-app prompts, NPS surveys, support tickets, quarterly interviews.
- Process: tag feedback by feature; log in backlog; review weekly.
- NPS: send quarterly; track trend; follow-up on detractors.
- Calls: schedule 3 customer calls/month; share notes in product board.
- Close loop: notify customers when feedback shipped.
