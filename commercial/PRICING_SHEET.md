# Pricing (APP_ID)

Assumptions: billed monthly in USD; usage bands per month.

| Tier | Contractors | Storage (GB) | API calls | Price | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | up to 50 | 50 | 1M | $499 | Email support, shared infra |
| Business | 51-250 | 200 | 5M | $1,499 | SSO add-on $300; priority support |
| Enterprise | 251+ | 1TB | 20M | Custom | Dedicated support, custom SLAs |

Add-ons: payroll module ($0.30/contractor/mo), integrations ($200+), premium support ($500), SSO ($300), extra storage/API on overages per GB/1M calls.

Example bills: Starter @40 contractors +20GB ~ $499; Business @120 contractors +50GB + SSO ~ $1,799.
Trial: 14 days, limited to 10 contractors.
