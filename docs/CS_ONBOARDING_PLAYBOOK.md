# CS Onboarding Playbook

## First 30 days
- Day 0: Send welcome email; share quickstart docs.
- Day 1-3: Guide to add employees and contractors; schedule training.
- Day 4-7: Post and approve first job; verify payouts setup.
- Day 8-14: Review reporting; introduce support channels.
- Day 15-30: Quarterly business review placeholder.

## Escalation triggers
- No login in 7 days.
- No job posted in 10 days.
- First ticket unresolved > 48h.

## SLA
- First response: within 4 business hours.
- Resolution target: 2 business days for P1, 5 for P2.

## Training agenda (sample)
- Platform overview
- Job lifecycle
- Approvals and payouts
- Reporting and exports
- Support and escalation
