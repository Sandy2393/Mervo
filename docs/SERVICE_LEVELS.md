# Service Levels (SLA)

## Tiers
- **Basic**: 99.0% uptime target; Response: 48h biz hours; Channels: email. Included: docs, community. Onboarding: 2h Q&A. Price: $0–$199/mo.
- **Standard**: 99.5% uptime; Response: 8h (biz); Critical 2h; Channels: email + Slack; Onboarding: 6h guided. Price: $499–$1,499/mo.
- **Premium**: 99.9% uptime; Response: 1h 24/7; Critical 30m; Channels: phone + Slack + email; Onboarding: 12h + custom. Price: $2,999–$7,999/mo.

## Credits / Refunds
- Breach of uptime SLO in a month → service credit: 5% (Basic), 10% (Standard), 20% (Premium) of monthly fee.
- Response-time breach for Critical incidents → 5% credit for Standard, 10% for Premium.
- Credits apply to next invoice; require customer request within 30 days. No cash refunds.

## Escalation SLA
- Critical (P1): Premium 30m response/4h target restore; Standard 2h/12h; Basic best-effort.
- High (P2): Premium 1h/24h; Standard 4h/48h; Basic best-effort.
- Medium (P3): Standard 1 biz day; Premium same; Basic best-effort.

## Included Features by Tier
- Basic: help center, status page, monthly release notes.
- Standard: + Slack support, quarterly business review, basic integrations help.
- Premium: + dedicated CSM, custom integrations, priority release window, enterprise SSO add-on support.
