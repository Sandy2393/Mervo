# Pricing Tiers

## Table
| Tier | Monthly | Uptime Target | Response | Included | Add-ons |
|------|---------|---------------|----------|----------|---------|
| Basic | $0–$199 | 99.0% | 48h biz | Docs, community | N/A |
| Standard | $499–$1,499 | 99.5% | 8h biz / 2h critical | Slack, quarterly review, guided onboarding | Payroll integration, SSO |
| Premium | $2,999–$7,999 | 99.9% | 1h 24/7 / 30m critical | Dedicated CSM, custom integrations, priority window | Enterprise SSO, dedicated infra |

## Assumptions
- Avg contractors: 50 (Basic), 250 (Standard), 1,000 (Premium)
- Storage/month: 50 GB, 250 GB, 1 TB
- Images/month: 5k, 25k, 100k
- Overages billed at published rates (TODO: set rates)

## Add-Ons
- Payroll integration: $299/mo + $0.50/job
- Priority support channel: $499/mo
- SSO/Enterprise: $999/mo + setup
- Dedicated infra: $1,999+/mo

## Example Invoices
1) Small (Basic): 20 contractors, 10 GB storage → $99/mo.
2) Mid (Standard): 200 contractors, 150 GB storage, payroll add-on → $1,199 + $299 = $1,498/mo.
3) Large (Premium): 900 contractors, 800 GB storage, SSO + dedicated infra → ~$6,499/mo + $999 + $1,999 = ~$9,497/mo.

## Billing Cadence
- Monthly in advance; annual prepay discounts (10–15%).
- Usage overages billed monthly in arrears.
