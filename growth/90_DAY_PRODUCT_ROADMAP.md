# 90-Day Roadmap (APP_ID)

## First 30 days
- Stabilize: fix launch bugs, harden auth/storage.
- Metrics: track signups, job completion, error rate.
- Customers: onboard 3 pilot customers; weekly check-ins.

## 60 days
- Features: data export improvements, SSO polish, better photo uploads.
- Growth: launch case study; run retargeting campaign.
- Ops: optimize worker throughput; refine alerts.

## 90 days
- Expansion: add integrations (payroll/CRM), localization plan.
- Monetization: evaluate pricing tweaks, upsell to Business tier.
- Metrics: target NPS >40, job completion rate >92%.
